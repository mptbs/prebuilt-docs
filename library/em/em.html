em — Functions related to low power modes¶

The em module contains functions for entering low power modes, as well as classes for controlling some low power peripherals.

EM4 Wakeup¶

Currently both the em.Cryotimer and machine.Pin provide support for EM4 wakeup.

Interrupts¶

Callbacks used by functions and class methods of the em module. The callback should be considered to be running in interrupt context. See Writing interrupt handlers.

Sleep related functions¶

em.enter_em1()¶

Enter Energy Mode 1.

em.enter_em2()¶

Enter Energy Mode 2

em.enter_em3()¶

Enter Energy Mode 3

em.enter_em4h(wakeup_objects, gpio_retention)¶

Enter Energy Mode 4 Hibernate. The wakeup_objects parameter is a list of peripherals that should be allowed to wake up the controller. If gpio_retention is true, all GPIO pins are kept at their current state until waking up again.

em.enter_em4s(wakeup_objects, gpio_retention)¶

Enter Energy Mode 4 Hibernate. The wakeup_objects parameter is a list of peripherals that should be allowed to wake up the controller. If gpio_retention is true, all GPIO pins are kept at their current state until waking up again.
