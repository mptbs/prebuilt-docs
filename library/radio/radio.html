radio — Functions and classes related to radio operation¶

The radio module gives access to the Radio peripheral on the Thunderboard Sense. As of yet, only a class that acts as a wrapper around the Rail SDK exists.

Future versions might implement Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), Thread or Zigbee.
